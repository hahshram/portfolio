An approachable, hardworking student, Luis Ramuel Quiroz strives to create projects while learning and with excellent cooperation and communication with his teammates. Luis Ramuel grew up on a farm in the Philippines. There, he discovered the struggle of growing up and working hard. He moved to Canada in 2022 and studied Interactive Digital Media at Technical Vocational High School. And in that school, he also learned what it takes to do things as a team and how to communicate with his classmates. He adapted to the environment and became independent quickly. He gave importance to everyone more than himself. Luis Ramuel is excited about learning new things and experiencing brand-new knowledge to add to his own.
